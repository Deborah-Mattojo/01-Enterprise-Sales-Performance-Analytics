# Enterprise Sales Performance Analytics Dashboard

## Overview

This project explores enterprise-wide sales performance using the Adventure Works Cycles dataset to identify revenue drivers, evaluate product performance, assess territory contribution, and generate business insights that support strategic decision making.

The project was developed as an end-to-end business intelligence solution using Power BI, combining data modelling, KPI development, interactive dashboard design, and business-focused analytical reporting.

---

## Business Context

Organizations operating across multiple products, territories, and sales teams require visibility into performance drivers in order to optimize revenue generation and support sustainable growth.

This analysis was designed to identify the factors influencing overall business performance and uncover strategic opportunities for operational improvement.

---

## Project Objectives

This analysis focused on:

* Evaluating overall revenue performance
* Identifying top performing product categories
* Comparing sales performance across territories
* Assessing salesperson effectiveness
* Identifying key revenue drivers influencing growth

---

## Dataset Information

Dataset Used: Adventure Works Cycles Dataset

The dataset contains historical business transaction records covering sales activity between 2011 and 2014.

The original dataset contained multiple business tables including sales, purchasing, customer, contact, HR and product data. Relevant tables were carefully reviewed and filtered before selecting the most business-critical datasets for analysis.

## Methodology

The project followed the following analytical workflow:

* Reviewed and filtered source tables for relevant business data
* Performed data cleaning and transformation using Power Query
* Built relationships across multiple business entities
* Developed a structured relational data model
* Created DAX measures for KPI calculations
* Built interactive dashboards for executive reporting and performance analysis

---

## KPIs Developed

* Total Revenue
* Total Orders
* Average Order Value
* Total Customers
* Sales Year-to-Date
* Quota Achievement Percentage

---

## Key Insights

The analysis revealed several important business insights:

* The United States was the strongest performing market and largest revenue contributor
* Australia demonstrated strong revenue efficiency despite comparatively lower order volume
* Bikes and Components were the organization’s highest revenue generating product categories
* Internally manufactured products generated the overwhelming majority of total revenue
* Salesperson performance trends revealed both consistently strong performers and emerging top contributors

---

## Recommendations

Based on analysis, the following strategic recommendations were identified:

* Continue strategic investment in high-performing markets such as the United States
* Investigate factors driving Australia’s strong revenue efficiency for possible expansion
* Maintain focus on high-performing product categories while identifying cross-selling opportunities
* Analyze and replicate successful strategies used by top-performing sales teams
* Continue monitoring territory-level performance trends to support growth planning
